Setup Instructions

  1. Open Claude.ai and go to your account settings
  2. Navigate to "Custom Connectors" or "MCP Settings"
  3. Click "Add Custom Connector"
  4. Enter the URL below as your connector endpoint
  5. Save and enable the connector
  6. Start using CSG Portal tools in your Claude conversations!

First Time Setup

After adding the connector to Claude.ai:

  1. Ask Claude: "Set me as the default user with email: your-email@example.com"
  2. Ask Claude: "Please log me in to the CSG portal"
  3. A browser window will open for secure authentication
  4. Enter your Veracross credentials
  5. You're ready to use all portal features!
